Shortly after completing my undergrad work, earning a Bachelor of Arts degree in English, I began a career in Law Enforcement. I served in multiple roles throughout my time in Law Enforcement, including: Field Training Officer, SWAT Operator, Patrol Sergeant and Investigations Sergeant. I received specialized training in officer safety tactics, basic life support, active shooter response, mental health response, interview and interrogation, sexual assault investigations, financial crimes investigations, as well as supervisory and leadership training. During this time, I also completed a post-graduate program through Sam Houston State University, earning a Masters in Science Criminal Justice Leadership and Management degree. Due to a work-related injury, I was unable to continue performing the duties required to remain an active law enforcement officer and was forced to seek early retirement. And so, my journey into coding and cybersecurity begins...
